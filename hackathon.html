About our Hackathon

Our event will be taking place from April 10-11th remotely through Zoom. This hackathon is focused on web design so our participants will be creating even deploying their vey own web applications online! The applications will be worked on individually but we do encourage parents to assist Juniors with the process.

Who can register?

Anyone can register regardless of experience! Our Juniors category will be ages 7-13 and Seniors are 14+ so don't feel intimidated to sign up even if you are a beginner. Our volunteers will be there to assist you with set up and any questions you have.

We're looking for volunteers!

If you want to get valuable leadership experience or are interested in Web Design / Computer Science this is an excellent oppurtunity to get some hands on experience helping beginners in the field.
